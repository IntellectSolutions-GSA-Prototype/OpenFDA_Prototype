Beta Usage

ADERS and openFDA are beta research project and not for clinical use. We may limit or otherwise restrict your access in accordance with FDA Terms of Use.
The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) maintains a repository of medications, adverse effects and recalls as part of the OpenFDA initiative. Intellect Solutions designed a new website - Adverse Drug Effects Research System (ADERS) - to allow individuals to securely utilize this website to research the adverse effects reported for medications.

To begin, please use the Drug Search menu option or click here to research medications.

To see how we came up with ADERS prototype solution, please use the Our Solution menu option or click here to watch the video.
